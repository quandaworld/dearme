About DearMe

DearMe is a digital time capsule application that lets you preserve memories for your future self.

Our Story

DearMe was created with a simple idea in mind: to help people connect with their past and future selves. In a world where we're constantly moving forward, we sometimes forget to pause and reflect on our journey. DearMe provides a space for that reflection.

How It Works

Create digital time capsules by adding text, images, links, and other content. Set a future date when you want to open your capsule. When that date arrives, you'll receive a notification that your capsule is ready to be opened, allowing you to revisit your past thoughts and memories.

Our Features

Private Capsules

Keep your thoughts completely private, or choose to share them with friends or make them public.

Timeline View

View all your opened capsules in a chronological timeline to see how you've changed over time.

On This Day

Discover capsules you created on this day in previous years.
